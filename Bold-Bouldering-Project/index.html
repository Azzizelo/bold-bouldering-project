🌍 Popular Bouldering Areas in Ireland

Doolin 🪨

Limestone seaside bouldering with views across to the Aran Islands. Great mix of easy and hard routes.

The Scalp 🌄

Located near Dublin in Wicklow, The Scalp features rugged terrain, steep climbs, and stunning granite.

Black Valley 🌿

Beginner-friendly routes in the peaceful and scenic heart of Killarney National Park, Kerry.

Wicklow Mountains 🏔️

Diverse bouldering routes in the highlands of Wicklow. Stunning landscapes and serious climbs.
