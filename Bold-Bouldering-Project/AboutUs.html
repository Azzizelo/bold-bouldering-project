About Bold Bouldering

Bold Bouldering is a passion project created by a group of Irish outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for bouldering across the Emerald Isle. From rugged coastlines to serene valleys, we explore, photograph, and document the most exciting climbing spots available to the public.
